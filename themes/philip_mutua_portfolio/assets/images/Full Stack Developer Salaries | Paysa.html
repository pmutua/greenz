Market Salary

Close
Paysa Market Salary is the total annual salary paid to employees through a mixture of base salary, annual bonus, equity, and signing bonus.
Every company pays employees differently, and Paysa Market Salary estimates include each component of annual employee pay, calculated for each position based on data spanning 7.45 million job changes, across 198,000 companies. When you share your career details with Paysa we can also personalize any estimate for you, based on any position and how it matches your unique career.
Paysa Market Salary estimates consist of both a point estimate and range.
The point estimate or average represents the conditional mean estimate, which represents our best guess within a range. The range is a 75% confidence interval, where the 75% confidence interval is indicated by the lower and upper bound range estimates. This means that 75% of the time a given market salary will lie between the lower and upper bounds of the confidence interval.
